Eternal is a class 3 difficulty, located after Horrific, and before Unreal. To put it simply, it is extremely difficult to complete, and requires extensive knowledge of intricate glitches and mechanics found within Roblox’s obbies and towers.

Despite this being humanly possible, and towers of this difficulty being currently feasible for any obbyists (due to every spectrum of this difficulty being possible), it is recommended any player willing to attempt this difficulty utilizes Tool Assisted Speedrun while trying to beat this. This is because it makes the obstacles significantly easier, and they won’t spend hundreds (if not thousands) of hours on towers of this difficulty.

Currently, Eternal additionally reflects obstacles seen in skyline Horrific and belowground Unreal, despite being listed as between the two.

Current Icon[]

Eternal’s current icon is represented by a purplish pink border, with a black background filling the rest of the icon. It’s rather simple.

Old Icon[]

Eternal’s icon is represented by a completely black background, and there is a pale red gradient at the bottom half. There is a pale >_X emoticon, slightly rotated, and with a thin, dark pale orange outline. There is a comcentrated orange text, behind that emoticon, saying the words ‘HELL’ in capital letters. In the top left corner, there is a hand-drawn X_X emoticon, tilted. Finally, I’m the top right corner, with the same appearance as the ‘HELL’ text, there is a small ‘MASSACRE’ tests.

Oldest Icon[]

Eternal’s oldest icon is represented by a black background and a purple border, with white corners.

Original Icon[]

Eternal’s oldest icon has a purple background, with a gradient at the top half going downwards from dark purple to the aforementioned purple color. In the middle, there is a worried face, with the outlines being white. Finally, the corners are completely white.

Trivia[]

  • During development, Eternal’s icon received a revamp to make it in the style of the current Horrific and Unreal icons. It became the old JToH style icon, however that decision was quickly reverted.
  • This difficulty was revamped to try and save it from being deleted in an omnicide, albeit the icon was the problem for Eternal.
  • On the 5th August 2022, this difficulty became a sub difficulty of Horrific, as per the Class 1-3 cleanup. However, Confident was still between Horrific and Unreal.
    • Due to this, Eternalized was swapped with Confident, and became a non-sub difficulty once again. This occurred on the 1st March 2023 (along with its revamp), almost 7 months after the initial change of placement.
  • A second revamp was put into place for this difficulty approximately nine months after the initial revamp date, and nearly ten months after it’s initial creation date, to bring it up to current JToH's Joke Towers Wiki standards.
  • This is likely vira’s most detailed and comprehensive article made on this wiki.
  • This difficulty was formerly the world record for the JJT Pyramid, set by AgileGamez.
  • On 5th August 2024, this difficulty was renamed to Eternal, due to the fact that Eternalized generally is not a good name for this kind of difficulty
  • There was a difficulty also named Eternal in C24, but it got deleted.

Notes

The difficulty tabs always open downwards.

Keep in mind that this navbox was not designed for light mode.

Note that some of the links are broken or redirects of another page.

Explanation

All difficulties are organized by class and spectrum including sub difficulties. Unclassified difficulties are excluded from this rule.

The Main Difficulty Chart is spilt into two parts: Main Chart and Extended Chart.

Difficulties with long names will get abbreviated. For example, Ifinite-Ifinite-Easy-Ifinite-Easy-Alpha-Easy-Null-Ifinite: Revamp.

Some of the chains are incorporated into the main chart. For example, the Aleph Sequence was integrated into the whole Class 11's chart in attempt to improve connectivity.

Chains with one page are not added to the navbox. The Cat Chain is an example of incorporating all difficulties to form a single page.

Unions will become regular difficulties and placed on their respective classes.

Primordial means difficulties that break up the whole spectrum of the Main Difficulty Chart. This consists five difficulties placed at their respective positions.

Legendary means difficulties earns high respect by the community are ranked as legendary difficulties as they are designed well. These difficulties must be accepted by Nugly in order to become legendary.

Fabled mean that difficulties define new importance and uniqueness to the Main Difficulty Chart.

Ethereal is reserved only for Babass dficuklty, and means that the difficulty is more omnipotent than any other difficulty on the Difficulty Chart.

Former Legendary mean that difficulties used to be legendary are demoted to low position due to quality issues and arguments.

Unclassified means that the difficulty is not placed any class on the Difficulty Chart. It can range from a few to many classes.

Unknown means the difficulty has an unknown placement on the difficulty spectrum and does not appear on the Difficulty Chart.
